# Chrysantha — Product Context

## Register

**Product** — dashboard, tool. Design serves the product. Data legibility and cognitive clarity outweigh aesthetic expression.

## Users

A single user managing personal wealth plus entrusted family assets across Chinese A-shares, Japanese yen, Hong Kong dollar, Alipay, WeChat Pay, and bank accounts. No multi-user collaboration. No admin panel needed. Used primarily on desktop (27-inch monitor) in the evening, with occasional mobile checks.

## Product Purpose

Unified asset dashboard that breaks platform barriers (WeChat / Alipay / bank / broker) for one-glance net worth visibility. Three core jobs:

1. **全景视图** — See everything in one place, denominated in CNY with live FX conversion
2. **风险控制** — Instantly distinguish liquid reserves from invested capital from entrusted funds
3. **代管透明** — Track managed family assets separately without operational overhead

## Brand Personality

- Calm, restrained, quietly authoritative. Like a Bloomberg Terminal stripped of noise.
- Data-first, not decoration-first. Every pixel earns its place.
- Warm but professional — not cold bank-lobby marble, not startup-landing-page bright.
- Trustworthy through precision, not through heavy-handed "security" signifiers (no padlock icons, no shields).

## Anti-References

- Not 同花顺 / 东方财富 — no red-green flicker, no information overload, no crowded tables
- Not SaaS dashboard template — no navy sidebar + white card grid
- Not crypto dark mode — no neon-on-black, no glassmorphism
- Not Robinhood — no gamification, no confetti, no "you're doing great!" copy

## Strategic Design Principles

1. **Information, not decoration.** A number well-placed beats a chart poorly-conceived. Remove before adding.
2. **One screen, one question.** The overview answers "how am I doing?" The buffer card answers "am I safe?" No scrolling to find answers.
3. **Color means something.** Green = buffer/reserve, orange = entrusted, blue = investment. Color is a data channel, not decoration. Never use color without a semantic anchor.
4. **Numbers are the interface.** Values in CNY, cleanly formatted, with appropriate precision. Currency conversion is invisible. The user sees one number in one currency.
5. **Respect the evening.** Designed for low-ambient-light use. Not bright white. Not pitch black. A warm, comfortable reading surface.
